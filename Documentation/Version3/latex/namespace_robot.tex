{\footnotesize\ttfamily enum \mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_robot_a889b5c1b2dca7377fc076c842119c833}{Robot\+::leg\+Status}}}



An enum to define the concept of LEG STATUS in the program. A robot\textquotesingle{}s total path is divided into the first half of the trip where it MOVES and the second half where it PUSHES the box. THe PUSH half is further divided into two halves, the part in which the robot is pushing to the first \char`\"{}dog leg\char`\"{} or right angle waypoint, and the second half where it is simply pushing in a straight line to the door. The variable ON\+\_\+\+MOVE\+\_\+\+OR\+\_\+\+FIRST\+\_\+\+LEG\+\_\+\+PUSH describes just that, and ON\+\_\+\+SECOND\+\_\+\+LEG describes the final push to the door. Sometimes, A robot will already be in the push position to go straight to the door and will have NO\+\_\+\+SECOND\+\_\+\+LEG.
